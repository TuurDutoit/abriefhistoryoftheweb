A Brief History of the Web

A story about communication.

Once upon a time...
scroll, swipe, keys

...there was nothing.

Well, no computers anyway.

Data was collected and processed by hand, text was typed with a typewriter and letters and telegram were the de-facto communication methods.

19xx

Until Alexander Graham Bell invents the telephone. He founds Bell Labs, a company that will run the phone lines for the forseeable future and will - as we now know - play a vital role in our history.
